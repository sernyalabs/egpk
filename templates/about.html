About eGPK

eGPK (e-Grievance and Public Karya Portal) is a digital initiative aimed at streamlining public grievances and community development tracking for 53-Ralong Namlung. Our goal is to create a transparent, accountable, and accessible platform for citizens and administrators alike.

This platform allows users to submit grievances, monitor public projects, and stay updated on community affairs. Our vision is to empower rural administration with the tools of modern technology.
